About Melvera

The Royal Federation of Melvera (or simply Melvera)is a nation located in the northwest quadrant (-,-) of CivMC.

It is a tundra nation on the continent of Northlandia. It was founded on July 23rd, 2025, following the ratification of an agreement with the Pirate Queen of Nassau, Paddington_Bear.
